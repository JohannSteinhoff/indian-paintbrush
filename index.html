The story

A little wildflower, a lot of heart.

The Indian paintbrush grows wild across the western hills, its tips dipped in scarlet like a brush pulled straight from the can. It felt like the right name for what we do — bringing bold, natural color to the places people call home.

What started as weekend jobs for neighbors has grown into a trusted local painting business. We're still small on purpose: it means you work directly with the person holding the brush, and nothing gets rushed.

The process

Simple, from hello to the last coat.

  1. 01

    Walkthrough

    We visit, listen to what you want, and take measurements — no pressure, no charge.

  2. 02

    Color & quote

    You get a clear written estimate and help picking colors and finishes.

  3. 03

    Prep & protect

    We cover floors and furniture, patch, sand, and prime so the paint truly lasts.

  4. 04

    Paint & polish

    Careful coats, a final walkthrough together, and a spotless space left behind.
